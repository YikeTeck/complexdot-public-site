About

System SRL is an Italian company specializing in the development and provision of management software solutions and integrated IT services for the national market. Our offerings include customizable and scalable business management software for companies of various sizes, from small businesses to medium-sized industries, as well as specific solutions for accounting firms. We also handle personnel management, including systems for payroll, attendance, and vehicle fleets. Beyond software, we provide professional cybersecurity services, hosting, housing, email server management, and other network services. We offer hardware support, corporate network installation, and training courses. To extend our coverage across Italy, we collaborate with a network of partners, including Sicom srl for accounting software, and participate in development consortia such as the one that created the ManGo software.
