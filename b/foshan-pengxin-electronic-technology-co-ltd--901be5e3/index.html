About

Foshan Pengxin Electronic Technology Co., Ltd. is a dedicated Electronic Manufacturing Services (EMS) provider operating from a modern facility in the CIFI Smart Manufacturing Centre, Foshan. The company integrates advanced production technology with a comprehensive one-stop processing model to deliver high-precision electronic assemblies. Its core capabilities are built around automated surface-mount technology (SMT) lines, lead-free soldering processes, and rigorous automated optical inspection (AOI), ensuring consistent product quality. Foshan Pengxin's electronic products and manufacturing services support diverse, technology-driven markets including industrial automation, medical devices, security and access control systems, biometrics (face recognition), public broadcasting, and entertainment gaming equipment.

Services & Capabilities

The company operates a state-of-the-art production facility featuring fully automatic printers, high-speed pick-and-place machines, lead-free reflow and wave soldering systems, automatic insertion machines, and advanced AOI (Automated Optical Inspection) equipment. This comprehensive setup ensures precision at every stage and delivers high-quality finished products. The core philosophy emphasizes technological capability and rigorous quality control as fundamental drivers of business growth and customer satisfaction.
