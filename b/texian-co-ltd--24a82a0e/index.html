About

Shenzhen Outdoor Special Display Equipment Co., Ltd. is a specialized provider focused on the design, manufacturing, and integration of outdoor special display equipment. Based in Guangming New District, Shenzhen, the company operates a 3,000-square-meter modern factory with over 50 employees. It is equipped with advanced machinery including large laser cutters, high-precision CNC punching machines, CNC bending machines, digital hydraulic shears, CNC milling machines, and spray testing facilities, enabling complete in-house production from sheet metal processing to final assembly. The company's core offerings encompass commercial display equipment, outdoor high-brightness display sheet metal, customized advertising kiosks, and splicing monitor kits. It provides a comprehensive one-stop service covering design and R&D, OEM manufacturing, painting, and full integration of outdoor high-brightness display systems. With dedicated teams for sheet metal engineering, display integration, thermal management, waterproofing, and safety design, the company serves as a reliable long-term partner in the digital signage industry.

Services & Capabilities

The company's services span industries including outdoor vending machines, smart electronic bus stops, outdoor charging piles, outdoor advertising displays, intelligent transportation, community public displays, tourist attraction public displays, taxi public display equipment, unattended supermarkets, outdoor retail equipment, and various industrial control display devices resistant to high and low temperatures. Adhering to a 'win-win cooperation, mutual benefit' philosophy, the company is dedicated to providing high-quality products and services to its clients.
